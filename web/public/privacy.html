Arbor Mobile Privacy Notice

Last updated: 2026-06-20

This operational notice documents the data categories used by Arbor Mobile. It must be reviewed by the product owner and legal/privacy owner before public store submission.

Data Used By The App

  • Account identifiers and role information for sign-in and permissions.
  • Job, schedule, branch, equipment, material, and worklog records.
  • Photos, camera captures, and uploaded evidence attached to work items.
  • Location information used for crew scheduling and field operations.
  • Diagnostics, crash reports, and error details when monitoring is enabled.

Why Data Is Used

Arbor Mobile uses this data to authenticate field workers, show assigned jobs, document work, support offline field workflows, synchronize job status, and help operators investigate production incidents.

Data Sharing

Data is processed by the Arbor backend and infrastructure providers used to operate the product. Crash or error data may be sent to the configured monitoring destination when production monitoring is enabled.

Retention And Deletion

Retention and deletion rules are managed by the workspace owner and backend administrator. Store submission must confirm the retention policy and the process for account or workspace data requests.

Support

For privacy or support questions, contact the Arbor release owner or workspace administrator. See Arbor Mobile Support.
