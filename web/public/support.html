Arbor Mobile Support

Last updated: 2026-06-20

Arbor Mobile supports field teams using the Arbor operations platform. This page gives reviewers, testers, and production users a stable support destination for the mobile app.

Help With Access

If you cannot sign in, ask your Arbor workspace administrator to confirm that your account is active, assigned to the correct branch, and has the expected field-worker permissions.

Operational Issues

For job, schedule, GPS, photo, or offline queue issues, include the user account, approximate time, job identifier, device model, operating system, and whether the device was online.

Store Review

App review accounts and demo data are prepared by the release owner before TestFlight, App Store review, or Google Play internal testing. Reviewers should use only the credentials supplied in the submission notes.

Contact

Contact the Arbor release owner or workspace administrator for support. Production support ownership must be confirmed before store submission.
